Event Details

CCIET is a distinctive networking and exploratory journey from Phnom Penh to Sihanoukville. This touring aims to connect key decision-makers from Cambodia and China. It will provide a platform for networking, sponsor branding, and government collaboration. Focused on investment exploration in Sihanoukville, it offers attendees a unique opportunity to witness the city’s tourism growth and investment prospects firsthand, encouraging cooperation between Cambodian and Chinese businesses through site visits and networking activities.

Why You Should Register

By registering for this specialized tour, you gain exclusive, firsthand access to the high-growth sectors defining Preah Sihanouk's future, including:
• Logistics & Transport: Capitalizing on its status as the nation's only deep-sea port, alongside key infrastructure like the Phnom Penh-Sihanoukville Expressway.
• Industrial & SEZ Development: Exploring the rapidly expanding Sihanoukville Special Economic Zone (SSEZ) and new industrial zones for high-tech and agro-processing.
• Integrated Urban Development & Tourism: Identifying opportunities in the planned smart city zones and high-end coastal tourism projects.
• Networking Opportunities: Connect directly with government representatives, industry leaders, and investors to build valuable partnerships and explore future collaborations.
